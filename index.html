Research Interests

Multimodal AI in Healthcare

  • Causal Inference for ICU Interventions: Designing transformer-based architectures to estimate treatment effects and mitigate confounding in electronic health records, integrating structured EHR data, clinical notes, and physiological signals.
  • Missing Modality Imputation: Developing multimodal deep learning systems to detect and fill missing data modalities in patient records, enhancing representation quality and predictive performance in critical care applications.

AI for Societal Systems

  • Game-Theoretic Policy Design: Modeling strategic interactions among governments, firms, and labor in autonomous taxi adoption, using dynamic games to inform equitable and sustainable policy decisions.
  • Sustainable System Forecasting: Building explainable ensemble models (XGBoost, DNN, RF) for CO₂ emissions and storm surge prediction, enabling data-driven planning in climate and transportation systems.
